Rutu Mulkar-Mehta, PhD

Founder and Data Scientist

Dr. Rutu Mulkar-Mehta founded Ticary Solutions LLC, in 2014, after receiving numerous requests for custom NLP solutions. Rutu brings her experience and expertise in Information Extraction, Topic Modeling, Recommender Systems, Word Semantics, and Machine Learning (among other things) to the company.

Rutu is very passionate about increasing the representation of women in technology. You can often find her speaking at conferences and events sporting her trademarked yellow scarf.
