Want to work with us? Here is the process that we follow:

(1) Discovery Call

Prior to an engagement, we will have a quick discussion to talk about your high level requirements, and evaluate the best step forward.

(2) Sign Master Services Agreement

Our MSA covers non-disclosure, indemnity and other basic requirements that sets the stage to get into a formal engagement.

(3) Requirement Analysis

We will have a series of deep discussions, and look at your data, and identify a list of requirements for the problem space.

(4) Project Proposal

This is a paid engagement, where we will develop a customized proposal for you, with recommended techniques and time and budget estimates.

(5) Statement of Work

Once an agreement is reached in our proposal, we will mutually sign an SOW that describes the work to be performed, milestones, and success criteria.

(6) Solution Development

We will work on your problem and develop tools and solutions, meeting at checkpoints as agreed upon in our SOW.

(7) Final Code Delivery

The final code will be delivered to you at the end of our engagement.

(8) Extended Support

Deliverables come with one year of free technical support, as highlighted in the SOW.

About us

Description

Ticary Solutions is a full stack software development consultancy that provides custom software solutions pertaining to Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, which are branches of Artificial Intelligence. We have 25+ years of experience in Software Development, and 15+ years of experience working in Artificial Intelligence (particularly NLP and ML).

Ticary Solutions is run by Dr. Rutu Mulkar-Mehta, who has a Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Southern California in Los Angeles, a Postdoc in Biomedical Informatics from University of California in San Diego, and is a part time faculty member at Northeastern University in Seattle.

We love a challenge, and solving hard problems is our specialty. We enjoy the creative process of coming up with out of the box problems, and the challenge of viewing problems differently.

We belive that each unique business deserves their own custom solutions in order to extract maximum value from their data. Standard off the shelf solutions can only take you part of the way there (learn why). Our custom solutions are designed to focus on your specific problems for maximum accuracy and productivity.
