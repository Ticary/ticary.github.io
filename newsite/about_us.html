About us

Ticary Solutions is a full stack software development consultancy that provides custom software solutions pertaining to Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, which are branches of Artificial Intelligence. We have 25+ years of experience in Software Development, and 15+ years of experience working in Artificial Intelligence (particularly NLP and ML).

Ticary Solutions is run by Dr. Rutu Mulkar-Mehta, who has a Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Southern California in Los Angeles, a Postdoc in Biomedical Informatics from University of California in San Diego, and is a part time faculty member at Northeastern University in Seattle.

We love a challenge, and solving hard problems is our specialty. We enjoy the creative process of coming up with out of the box problems, and the challenge of viewing problems differently.

We belive that each unique business deserves their own custom solutions in order to extract maximum value from their data. Standard off the shelf solutions can only take you part of the way there (learn why). Our custom solutions are designed to focus on your specific problems for maximum accuracy and productivity.
