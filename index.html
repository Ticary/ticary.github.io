About us


Ticary Solutions is a Natural Language Processing consultancy that provides full-stack software solutions. We have 25+ years of experience in Software Development, and 15+ years of experience working in Artificial Intelligence (particularly Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning).

Ticary Solutions is run by Dr. Rutu Mulkar-Mehta, who has a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Southern California in Los Angeles, a Postdoc in Biomedical Informatics from University of California in San Diego, and is a part-time faculty member at Northeastern University in Seattle.

We love a challenge, and solving hard problems is our specialty. We enjoy the creative process of coming up with out of the box solutions, and the challenge of viewing problems differently.

We believe that each unique business deserves their own custom solutions in order to extract maximum value from their data. Standard off the shelf solutions can only take you part of the way there (learn why). Our custom solutions are designed to focus on your specific problems for maximum accuracy and productivity.
